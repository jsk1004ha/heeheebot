Three built-in image generation prompts were used for the same fantasy blacksmith NPC concept:

3. DESTROY variant: comically apologetic but teasing, sweat drop, hand raised in apology, broken glowing sword shard and smoke.

Shared constraints:
- polished 2D game illustration
- anime-inspired fantasy RPG asset
- centered bust/half-body portrait
- high readability at Discord thumbnail size
- no text/logo/watermark
